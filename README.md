Walmart Sales Analysis

Project Overview : 

Retail businesses generate huge volumes of transactional data every day.
This project analyzes Walmart sales data to uncover important business insights such as sales trends, branch performance, product demand, and customer purchasing behavior.

The goal is to use data analysis to support better business decisions and identify opportunities to improve sales performance.


# Business Objectives

* Identify the **top performing Walmart branches**
* Analyze **sales trends over time**
* Determine the **best selling product categories**
* Understand **customer purchasing patterns**
* Identify factors contributing to **high sales revenue**



# Dataset Description

The dataset contains Walmart retail sales transaction records including:

* Branch
* City
* Customer Type
* Product Line
* Unit Price
* Quantity
* Total Sales
* Payment Method
* Customer Rating
* Date and Time of Purchase


# Data Analysis Process

Data Cleaning

* Checked for missing values
* Corrected data types
* Removed duplicate records

Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

* Which **branch generates the highest revenue?**
* Which **product line sells the most?**
* What are the **monthly and daily sales trends?**
* Which **payment methods are most commonly used?**
* How do **customer ratings relate to sales performance?**

# Visualizations

The project includes several visual insights:

* Sales distribution across branches
* Product line sales comparison
* Monthly revenue trends
* Payment method distribution
* Customer rating analysis


# Key Insights

* Some branches consistently outperform others in total revenue.
* Certain product categories contribute significantly to overall sales.
* Sales show peak periods during specific months and weekends.
* Digital payments are commonly preferred by customers.
