# Walmart Sales Analysis | Retail Performance & Trend Analytics

## Project Overview

Retail businesses generate large volumes of transactional and operational data that can be leveraged to improve decision-making and performance monitoring. This project analyzes Walmart sales data to identify sales trends, compare store performance, and evaluate the relationship between external economic factors and sales outcomes.

The analysis provides a structured view of retail performance and demonstrates how data can be used to support planning, forecasting, and operational decision-making.

---

# Business Problem

Retail organizations must continuously monitor sales performance across stores and time periods to understand growth opportunities and operational challenges.

Key questions addressed include:

* Which stores generate the highest sales?
* How do seasonal patterns affect revenue?
* Do economic factors influence sales performance?
* What trends can support business planning?

---

# Project Objectives

* Analyze sales performance across stores
* Identify seasonal and weekly sales trends
* Evaluate holiday sales behavior
* Assess relationships between sales and external factors
* Generate actionable business insights
